## 📞 Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q: What happens when I hit my limit?**
A: You'll receive a notification and can upgrade instantly. Your work is never interrupted - you just can't make new requests until you upgrade or the month resets.

### **Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes! Upgrade instantly, downgrade at end of billing period.

### **Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No. The price you see is the price you pay. No setup fees, no cancellation fees.

### **Q: What if I go over my limit?**
A: You'll be prompted to upgrade. We never charge overage fees - you simply can't exceed your limit without upgrading.

### **Q: Can I try Pro before buying?**
A: Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of Pro tier (coming soon).

### **Q: Do unused limits roll over?**
A: No, limits reset monthly. This keeps pricing simple and predictable.

### **Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Yes, 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.

### **Q: Is there a team plan?**
A: Yes! Enterprise tier includes team features. Contact us for custom team pricing.

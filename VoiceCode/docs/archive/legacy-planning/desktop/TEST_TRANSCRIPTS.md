The marketing team has done an exceptional job this quarter. We launched three major campaigns targeting small and medium-sized businesses in the technology and professional services sectors. The "Productivity Unleashed" campaign generated 2,500 qualified leads with a conversion rate of 12%, significantly higher than our historical average of 8%. The cost per acquisition decreased by 22% due to improved targeting and creative optimization.

The content marketing strategy focusing on thought leadership and educational content has resonated well with our target audience.

## 🚨 Important Notes

### Why Rebrand?
- **Trademark Conflict**: Voiceflow.com is an established company with potential trademark rights
- **Brand Confusion**: Users may confuse our product with their chatbot builder
- **Marketplace Risk**: VS Marketplace may reject extensions with similar names
- **SEO Competition**: Hard to rank for "voiceflow" searches

### Benefits of VoiceCode
- ✅ No trademark conflicts
- ✅ Clear purpose (voice + code)
- ✅ Professional branding
- ✅ Good SEO potential
- ✅ Easy to remember
- ✅ Emphasizes both transcription and coding features
